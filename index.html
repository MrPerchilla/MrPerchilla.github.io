The Gussinni family, renowned in the world of luxury and high-end services, has carved out a significant niche in the yachting industry.
The Vision: Turning Dreams into Design
 Every Gussinni yacht holds on the water like a goose. The process begins as a dream — a vision shared between the family and their discerning clients. Wealthy individuals, celebrities, and royalty come from across the globe, seeking not just a yacht, but a personalized expression of luxury and freedom.
Jean Pompendour
 Lord Jean Pompendour, a British nobleman with a long family history of environmental stewardship, selected Gussinni for its unparalleled craftsmanship and commitment to timeless design. His yacht, “Heritage,” embodies the grandeur of his ancestral estate, with interiors featuring mahogany wood paneling, antique furnishings, and an extensive library filled with rare books. Designed to host family retreats, the yacht includes a dedicated children's play area, a family cinema room, and an open-air deck perfect for hosting family celebrations. For Lord Pompendour, the yacht represents the continuity of his family's values — elegance, tradition, and the love of nature.
Family Heritage
 The choice of a Gussinni yacht is often made by distinguished family men who value tradition, legacy, and the enduring bonds of family heritage. These yachts are not merely vessels; they are floating sanctuaries that reflect the values of those who cherish time spent with loved ones. The Gussinni clientele is a curated circle of world-renowned business magnates, royals, and cultural icons who see their yachts as an extension of their family's story — family heritage.
